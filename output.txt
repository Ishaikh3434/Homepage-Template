
<p><strong>INSTRUCTIONS:</strong> Tasty Salad</p>
<p><strong>Ingredients:</strong></p>
<p><em>Salad:</em></p>
<ul>
<li>5 oz fresh lettuce, washed and chopped</li>
<li>1 medium tomato, diced</li>
<li>½ cup crumbled feta cheese</li>
<li>¼ cup thinly sliced red onion</li>
<li>2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil</li>
<li>1 tbsp fresh lemon juice</li>
<li>Salt and pepper to taste</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Method:</strong></p>
<p><em>Prepare Salad:</em></p>
<ol>
<li>In a large bowl, combine the chopped lettuce, diced tomatoes, crumbled feta cheese, and thinly sliced red onion.</li>
<li>In a small bowl, whisk together the extra virgin olive oil, fresh lemon juice, salt, and pepper.</li>
<li>Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.</li>
<li>Serve immediately.</li>
</ol>
